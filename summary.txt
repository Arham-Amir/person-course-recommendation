The Regulation, Quality, and Compliance in ADC Manufacturing training on September 10th and 11th, 2024, is a crucial two-day event for professionals in the Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs) industry. This course provides a deep dive into the complex manufacturing processes of ADCs, emphasizing the importance of a strong chemistry, manufacturing, and control (CMC) strategy to ensure success and avoid failures. Attendees will gain a comprehensive understanding of the technical and regulatory challenges specific to ADCs, including quality control, good manufacturing practices, and minimizing cross-contamination risks. Ideal participants include those in quality assurance, manufacturing, compliance, regulatory affairs, and operations, as well as VC/investors in Biologics. Professionals looking to enhance their knowledge and skills in ADC manufacturing and prepare for the unique challenges in this field would greatly benefit from attending this transformative training session.
